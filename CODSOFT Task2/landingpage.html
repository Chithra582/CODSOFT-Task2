    Ice Cream



    ABOUT

    Ice cream is a delicious frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings, enjoyed worldwide in various forms. It comes in flavors like vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry, with variations such as gelato, sorbet, and frozen yogurt. Originating from ancient civilizations, ice cream has evolved into a popular treat, available in cones, cups, and sundaes. Whether soft serve or classic scoops, its creamy texture and sweet taste make it a favorite for all ages. From street vendors to gourmet parlors, ice cream remains a symbol of indulgence and joy, perfect for any occasion.
